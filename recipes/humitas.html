Humitas

Humitas are made by grounding and cooking fresh corn and seasoning, wrapped in fresh corn husks, and steamed. The inside is hot, and the corn paste is mild in flavor.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Onions
  • Lard or butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Basil
  • Twine
