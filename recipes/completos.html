Chilean "Completo italiano"

The chilean "completo italiano" is without a doubt the king of Chilean fast food. It is named after the resemblance of it's ingredients of the colors in the italian flag.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bread
  • Sausages
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocados (ripe)
  • Mayonnaisse
  • Other sauces such as mustard or ketchup are optional.
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the sausages in a medium pot with boiling water. Boil over medium heat for 5 minutes. Wash, peel and dice the tomatoes into tiny squares.
  2. Split the avocados in halves with a knife. Remove the seed. Then use a fork to extract the content of the avocado into a bowl. Turn it into a paste with the fork and add some salt.
  3. Use a knife to slice the bread open. Preheat and oven to 100ÂºC and heat the buns.
  4. Put a sausage in each bun, cover with a layer of tomato and avocado on top. Finally, add a stripe of mayo on top of it all. You can add any other sauces if you prefer.
